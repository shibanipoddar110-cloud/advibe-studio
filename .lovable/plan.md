

# AdVibe — AI Video Ad Studio

A sleek, high-end single-page app for generating AI video ads with a premium dark/modern aesthetic.

## Design Direction
- Dark theme with vibrant accent colors (purple/blue gradients)
- Glassmorphism cards, smooth animations, and glowing CTAs
- Clean, spacious layout with a premium studio feel

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero Header
- "AdVibe" logo/wordmark with a subtle gradient
- Tagline: "Your AI-Powered Video Ad Studio"

### 2. Brand Soul Section
- Two styled inputs: **Website URL** and **Instagram Handle**
- **"Analyze Brand Vibe"** button that triggers a scanning animation with the text "Scanning aesthetics, colors, and tone..."
- After "analysis," show a mock result card with detected brand colors/tone

### 3. Ad Settings Section
- **Platform toggle**: Instagram Reels / Facebook Feed / YouTube Shorts (using a toggle group)
- **Duration selector**: 15s / 30s / Other — selecting "Other" reveals a small number input for custom seconds

### 4. The Magic Box
- Large textarea: "What are we selling today?"
- **"Generate Ad"** button with a pulsing/glowing gradient animation

All sections on a single scrollable page with fade-in animations as you scroll down.
